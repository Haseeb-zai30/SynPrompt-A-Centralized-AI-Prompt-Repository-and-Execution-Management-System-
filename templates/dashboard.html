SynPrompt is an AI-powered Prompt & Project Repository that enables users to create, organize, and manage prompts in one centralized workspace. Users can group prompts into projects, execute them using multiple AI models—including GPT, Gemini, and Claude—through OpenRouter, save favorite prompts, and maintain a complete execution history for future reference. Designed for developers, students, and AI enthusiasts, SynPrompt simplifies prompt engineering by making AI experimentation organized, searchable, and reusable.
